How it all started

Xalima was going to be a small project for me to learn how to code in JavaScript. Clearly this poject is not small at all. I decided I wanted to continue this project and make it public to everyone!


Why Xalima?

Xalima was originally going to be just a free private music bot. Later I decided to add a lot of freatures and make it public. Now Xalima serves as a 100% free, multi-purpose bot, and to be the only bot you will ever need!


Plans for Xalima?

The plans for Xalima at the moment is to add as many features as I can fit in the bot. Also to try and listen to the community as much as possible to get the best experiance out of the bot.
